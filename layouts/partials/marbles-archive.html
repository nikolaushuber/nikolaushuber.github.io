OCaml Marbles

On this site I want to collect different algorithms and datastructures implemented in the OCaml programming language.

Many similar sites/books exists for a variety of programming languages, and usually people call them things like Pyhon pearls, JavaScript Gems, ...

The reason I'm calling them marbles is simple. I do not claim that the programs I present here are the most elegant, or the most performant. They are not meant to be used in production, but rather should provide a playground for experimentation. And people are much more likely to play with marbles than with expensive jewellery 😉
